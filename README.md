# SuppTrack Privacy Policy

**Effective:** 4 July 2026

SuppTrack is designed so the developer does not receive your supplement or health-related information.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The app can store supplements, ingredients, schedules, dose history, inventory, prices, purchases, check-ins, notes, preferences, and reminder settings using Apple's local storage frameworks. These records remain on your device.

## Information Collected by Us

None.

SuppTrack has no account system, developer backend, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party analytics SDK. We do not receive your locally stored records.

## Purchases

Apple processes App Store purchases. SuppTrack receives entitlement information needed to unlock features. The developer does not receive payment card details.

## Notifications

If you grant permission, reminders are scheduled through Apple's local notification system. Permission is optional and can be withdrawn at any time through your device's Settings app.

## Exports

Data leaves the app only when you intentionally create an export and choose a destination through Apple's Share interface. After export, the privacy practices of the destination service apply.

## Retention and Deletion

Local records remain until you:

- Delete them within SuppTrack
- Use **Delete All Local Data**
- Remove the app from your device

The developer cannot retrieve or delete your records because they are never transmitted to us.

## iCloud

SuppTrack does not use CloudKit to synchronize health-related records.

## Children

SuppTrack is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy will be updated if the app's privacy practices change. The effective date will be revised, and App Store privacy disclosures will be updated where required.

## Contact

Please visit the **SuppTrack Support** page for contact information.
