STACKOS SUPPORT

How can we help?

Contact

Email [SUPPORT_EMAIL]. Do not email medical records, medication lists or other sensitive health information.

Restore a purchase

Open StackOS → Settings → Membership → Restore Purchases. Confirm the device uses the Apple Account that made the purchase.

Notifications

Open StackOS → Settings → Local Reminders. If permission was denied, enable notifications in the iPhone Settings app.

Export or delete data

Open StackOS → Settings → Data & Privacy. Exports are created only when requested. Delete All Local Data permanently removes app records.

Medical questions

StackOS support cannot answer medical questions. Contact an appropriate doctor, pharmacist or emergency service.
